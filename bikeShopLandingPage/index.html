Our Story

We started out traveling across country on a bike trip. One trip lead to another. Once we started doing so many trips we knew exactly what we wanted from a bike. So we started building our own. We fell in love with the difference a custom built bike makes and we fell in love with the process of making it. We started making bikes for our friends. And know we love making bikes for everyone.
